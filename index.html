District President's Message

Leo Sujeet Baishya

Warm greetings to all LEO Members and well wishers, I am very much thankful for you all for unanimously electing me as the founder district president of Leo District Council 325 N for the year 2022/23. I am humbled and honored to have been elected as a President and would like to thank all from the core of my heart for the enormous support and cooperation. I will take this opportunity as a valued recognition for which I along with my team will drive Leo's activities to set a new landmark.
